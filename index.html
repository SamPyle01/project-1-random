Project 1: Sticker Art Board

A simple p5.js sketch: click to place shapes or images, drag to move, keys to control.

How to Play

  • Click: place one random shape (circle/square/triangle) at the mouse.
  • Click & drag on an existing item: move it.
  • SPACE: clear all shapes/images.
  • B: change the background to a random color.
  • F: switch to Flowers...
  • S: switch to Sports ...
  • D: back to Shapes Mode.

Have fun!
